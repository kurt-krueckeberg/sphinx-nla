## Transliteration and Translation

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

NC. 4456
pr. in Cam 19/9 50

[3GR Stempel]

N.P. 1314
ad N.C. 4386

An Fürstliche Rentkammer Bericht des Amts Bückeburg

Verkauf der Krückebergschen Stätte No 18 in Berenbusch betr.

Bei Rücksendung des Gesuchs des Colons Krückeberg No 18 zu Evesen, um
Genehmigung eines über seine Stätte abgeschlossenen Kaufvertrags,
verfehlen wir nicht, Fürstlicher Rentkammer das in Abschrift angebogene
Protocoll vom 16. d. M. gehoramst vorzulegen und finden bei Gewährung des
Gesuchs Etwas nicht zu erinnern.

Bückeburg 17 Septbr. 1850.

Ditur consensus zum Verkaufe. Die angegeben Lasten waren zu revidiren
Bbg 19/9 50
Barkhausen
```

Translation

```{code-block} text

NC. 4456
received in the Chamber on 19/9/50

[3-groschen revenue stamp]

N.P. 1314
re: NC. 4386

Report to the Princely Rent Chamber from the Office of Bückeburg

Re: Sale of the Krückeberg holding (Stätte) No. 18 in Berenbusch

Upon returning the petition of Colon Krückeberg No. 18 of Evesen, requesting
approval of a purchase contract concluded for his holding,
we respectfully submit to the Princely Rent Chamber
the enclosed copy of the record of proceedings dated the 16th of the current month,
and we find that, if the petition is granted, there is nothing to object to.

Bückeburg, 17 September 1850.

Consent is given to the sale. The stated encumbrances are to be reviewed.
Bückeburg, 19/9/50
Barkhausen
```

Written in a smaller hand onto the bottom-left of the document are the
results of an **internal check by the Rentkammer** to make sure the fees and
obligations attached to the property were listed correctly before approving
its sale. It notes that **one fee was wrong** and that the others were correct.
