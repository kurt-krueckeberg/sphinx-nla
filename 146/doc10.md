Translation

```{code-block} text

No. 452.
[W?]ackelwer[k/t] (ambiguous surname)

The new settler Jobst Henrich Krückeberg, No. 18 in Berenbusch, has paid
into the chamber treasury the sum of seventeen Reichsthaler and three
groschen for a plot intended for house construction measuring 102 square
rods and 51 square feet.

Bückeburg,
20 December 1809.

Staackmann

17 Reichsthaler 3 groschen, in coin
```
