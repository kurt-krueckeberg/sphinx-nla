# Sale of the Krückeberg property no. 18 in Berenbusch to the shoemaker

Only the case file cover and the Abschrift (copy) of
[N.C. 4386](../146/carl-fried-supplication-to-sel) are included here. The
other documents include technical details of the sale, but are hard to
decipher due to their poor legibility. The important details are in the
[146:other-docs.xml](../146/other-docs.md) section of the case file [146:index.xml](../146/index.md),
which has all the relevant documents related to September 1850 sale of no.
18 Berenbusch.
