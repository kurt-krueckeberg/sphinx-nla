# Summary {#_summary .narrow}

The is the **Draft (Concept) of the official reply (Rescript) to the widow Krückeberg** It is the resolution to the petitioner: the prince has granted her three years' service excemption from the **Burgrest labor days** and the **forestry services**.

The text of the Office Reply appears on the top left (consisting of about three lines of text) and on the top right of the image.

    Concept

    To the widow Krückeberg No. 10 in Bärenbusch.

    By the highest authority, the widow Krückeberg, No. 10 in Bärenbusch,
    has been graciously granted exemption from Burgrest and forestry service
    for three years.

    Bückeburg, 4 August 1824.
    For the record.
    A. Kaas
