Translation

```{code-block} text

Draft
Re: Chamber No. 368.

Chamber rescript to the Office of Bückeburg.

Dispatched by Schwarze.

By the Grace of God.

The designation of land for the tenant Jobst Heinrich Krückeberg,
No. 18 at Bärenbusch, together with the obligations proposed by
you, has been approved. These obligations are to be paid for the
first time at Michaelmas 1813.

Bückeburg, 15 November 1809.

By authority of
von Danckwertz von Lindesberg
M. Kaas


Chamber instruction to the Chamber Rent Master Staackmann.

Dispatched.

To the new settler Jobst Heinrich Krückeberg, No. 18 at Bärenbusch,
a plot of land measuring approximately 390 square meters
(about 4,200 square feet) has been designated for the construction
of a house. The Chamber Rent Master is hereby instructed to collect
the purchase price for this designated plot at the established
rate, and to receive and account for the payment through the
appropriate office.

Bückeburg, mid-November 1809.

By order of the Princely Government
von Danckwertz von Lindesberg
M. Kaas

The total purchase price amounts to approximately
17⅛ Reichsthaler (that is, 17 Reichsthaler and 3 Groschen).
```
