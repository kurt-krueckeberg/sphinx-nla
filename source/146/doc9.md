# Summary {#_summary}

On December 7, 1809, Jobst Heinrich's wife had xref:146:doc8.adoc\[petitioned the Rentkammer\] to allow two payments instead of one for the plot of ground the Rentkammer xref:146:doc7.adoc\[had permitted to be alloted to Jobst Heinrich\], where he could build a house, plant a garden of crops and perhaps house a few animals.

:::: note
::: title
Note
:::

Payment for the tract of land, the **\"erhebliches Kolonat\"**, transferred only hereditary tenancy rights to the land not full ownership; ultimate title remained with the territorial authority.

::: title
Translation
:::

    Ref. no. 394
    Concept: Chamber Decree

    To the new settler Krückeberg
    No. 18 at Berenbusch

    Dispatched: Schwarze

    The petition submitted on the 7th of this month by the new settler Krückeberg,
    requesting installment-based payment for the plot of land assigned to him for
    house construction, is not granted.

    Bückeburg, 12 December 1809.

    F. St. g.

    By authority of Lindesberg
    M. Kaas
    Wandt
