    Chamber File No. 392
    Stamp: 3 Groschen

    [signatures / routing notations]

    Transmitted by: Knöttge

    To Colonist Krückeberg No. 10 at Bärenbusch

    The request of Krückeberg No. 10 in Bärenbusch, seeking a reduction in the
    price for the plot assigned to him on the Söhre, cannot be granted.

    Bückeburg, 7 October 1818.
    By order of the Princely authorities.
