# Petition of Colon Krückeberg for Permission to Sell his Farmstead (23 Aug. 1849)

## Document Summary

This document is the formal approval for the sale of Stätte (holding)
No. 10 in Berenbusch, which Colon Krückeberg has requested permission to
sell. Referring to the official report received from the Amt Bückeburg on
the 22nd of the month, the Princely Fiscal Chamber grants consent for the
sale. The approval is issued at Bückeburg on 23 August 1849 and is signed
by the Rentkammer official Spreng. Further clerical notations indicate
internal processing and routing (including “Exp” for dispatch).

```{note}
Meaning of the “Best. 1 — = 4x” Notation:

- **1 copy** is to be placed (Bestellt) in the official case file
- **4 copies** are to be made / issued (sent to the Amt, retained copies, or
used for internal distribution)

“Best. 1 — = 4x” is a Rentkammer copy-distribution mark. It is not
content; it is only an office filing directive.
```
