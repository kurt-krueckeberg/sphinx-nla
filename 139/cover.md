### Terms Explained

**Bauergüter** are long-established, legally defined farm holdings integrated
into the traditional village tenure system, whereas **Colonien** are later,
state-created settlement holdings carved out of forest or waste land and
governed initially by special founding contracts rather than long-standing
custom.

- **Colon** = legally defined settler of a state-founded Colonie
- **Bauer** = holder of a traditional Bauerngut, identified by role rather than title. *Bauer* is often implicit.
