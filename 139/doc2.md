# Actenstück 2 (Administrative File Item 2): Report from the Bückeburg Office (7 Aug. 1842)

## Report from the Bückeburg Office

This is a report written by the Amt Bückeburg (with Amt Arensburg
referenced, as usual in joint reporting) to the Rentkammer on 7 August
1824. It is responding to an earlier order from the Rentkammer dated 4 and
7 August 1824.

The report states that the widow Krückeberg, who holds Stätte (homestead)
No. 10 in Berenbusch, still possesses the piece of land “before the Söhre”
— land that her late husband had purchased from the Fürstliche Kammer for 8
Groschen Rthlr. (8/Carolins / 8 Reichsthaler Carolins, depending on
notation).

The Amts note that she still owns this land, though she has leased out part
of it to someone else.

The report is signed Sander at Bückeburg on 7 August 1824.

## Transliteration and Translation

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

N. 2935                    Præs: 8. Aug. 1824.
voriger Referent.

An Fürstliche Rentkammer Bericht der Aemter Bückeburg und Arensburg

Nr. C. 2816.          ad acta
Nr. pr. 698.

betr.
Stätte No 10. im
Bärenbusche.

Ad Rescriptum Clementissimæ de 4. et praes. 7. hujus berichten wir unter-
thänig, daß die Wittwe Krückeberg Colonia auf der rubricirten Stelle das
Stückland vor der Söhre, welches ihr verstorbener Ehemann für 8/C rthlr.
von Fürstlicher Kammer gekauft hat, jetzt noch besitzet. Einen Theil da-
von hat sie verpachtet.
Bückeburg 7. August 1824.

Sander
```

Translation

```{code-block} text

No. 2935                    Presented: 8 August 1824.
former rapporteur.

To the Princely Rent Chamber Report of the Offices of Bückeburg and
Arensburg

N.C. 2816.          filed
No. pr. 698.

re:
Farmstead No. 10 in
Bärenbusch.

In response to the Most Gracious official reply of the 4th and of the
present month’s 7th, we most humbly report that the widow Krückeberg,
tenant of the referenced holding, still possesses the parcel of land
near the Söhren which her late husband purchased from the Princely
Chamber for 800 rixthalers. A portion of it she has leased out.
Bückeburg, 7 August 1824.

Sander
```

```{note}
- **8/C** = "8 x 100" → 800. **8/C** was shorthand for 800.
```

## Comments of Document Identifiers

The document identifiers were written by:

- **N.C. 2816** — written by the *Amt* clerk, repeating the
Rentkammer’s file number for the ["Petition of the widow
Krückeberg, holding No.
10 in Berenbusch"](doc1-1.md).
- **Nr. pr. 698** — the *Amt*’s own internal protocol number for this
outgoing report.
- Both written in the *Amt* hand (Sander’s or his clerk’s).
- The **top-left Nr. 2935** and **Præs. 8 Aug 1824** were then added
*after receipt* by the Rentkammer registry.
