# Petition of Colon Krückeberg for Permission to Sell His Farmstead (8 August 1849)

## Narrative Summary

This document is a petition submitted by Colon Krückeberg of Stätte No. 10
in Berenbusch to the Princely Rentkammer. In it, Krückeberg states that he
has agreed on a sale price for his holding with the buyer, the Einlieger
Bornemann of Nordholz, and that the sale has already been reported to the
Amt. Because he plans to emigrate to America on September 15th, and still
needs time to sell his remaining household property and settle required
dues, he urgently requests that the Rentkammer issue its formal approval
for the sale as quickly as possible. The petition is dated 8 August 1849. A
note added by Barkhausen on 13 August instructs that the matter be
forwarded to the Amt Bückeburg for report and that a 12 Pfennig stamp fee
be collected and remitted.

## Transliteration and Translation

```{note}
This transliteration changed the arachic contraction "ichlso" to "ich also".
```

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

NC 4731                                 Jst. ml. 8/8 49.
Nr. pr. 1313                            Pr. am Amt 15/8 49
Stpl. 3 g.

[clerk's initials:] L W.

Eine untertänigste Bitte an die Hochfürstliche Rentkammer.

Ich bin willens, meine Kolonie zu verkaufen und nach Amerika auszuwandern
und bin mit dem Käufer, Einlieger Bornemann von Nordholz, wegen des
Kaufpreises einig, welches wir dem Amte angezeigt haben. Und da ich
willens bin, am 15ten September abzureisen, weil am 15ten September ein
Schiff zu meiner Reise abgeht und ich mein Inventarium noch zu verkaufen
habe und übrigens noch Abgaben zu entrichten habe; so bitte ich die
Hochfürstliche Rentkammer um schleunige Nachricht, so bald als möglich.
Untertänigst bitte ich also.

Untertänigster Kolon Krückeberg Nr. 10 in Berenbusch.
                                    d. 8ten August 1849.


NC 7. Tr. m. zum Berichte an das Amt Bückeburg. 12 mpt Stempelstrafe sind
einzuziehen und eingesendet.
Bbg. 13/8 49.
Barkhausen.
```

Translation

```{code-block} text

NC 4731. Registered 8 August 1849.
Registry No. 1313. Presented to the Amt on 15 August 1849.
Stamp fee: 3 Groschen.

A most humble petition to the High Princely Rent Chamber.

I have decided to sell my holding and to emigrate to America, and I am in
agreement with the buyer, the cottager Bornemann from Nordholz, regarding
the purchase price, which we have already reported to the Amt.  And since I
intend to depart on the 15th of September, because a ship for my journey
leaves on the 15th of September, and because I still must sell my inventory
and also settle the remaining obligations, I therefore request a prompt
reply from the High Princely Rent Chamber as soon as possible. I remain,
most humbly requesting accordingly.

Most humble colonist Krückeberg, No. 10 in Berenbusch.
Dated the 8th of August, 1849.

NC 7. Forwarded for report to the Amt Bückeburg. A stamp penalty of 12
Mariengroschen is to be collected and transmitted.
Bückeburg, 13 August 1849.
Barkhausen.
```
