# Report from the Amt Bückeburg Concerning the Sale of the Krückeberg Farmstead (22 August 1849)

## Narrative Summary

This document is a formal report sent from the Amt Bückeburg to the
Princely Fiscal Chamber in response to the petition of Colon Krückeberg of
Stätte No. 10 in Berenbusch, who has requested permission to sell his
holding. The Amt notes that it has received Krückeberg’s petition and
that the matter is confirmed in an attached protocol from the previous day.
The Amt states that it has no objections to the granting of the requested
permission. It further informs the Fiscal Chamber that Krückeberg intends
to emigrate to America and plans to depart as early as the 13th of the
coming month. The Amt therefore asks for a prompt decision from the Fiscal
Chamber. Finally, the Amt reports that, in accordance with an earlier
resolution dated the 13th of the month, it will collect a 12 Mariengroschen
stamp duty from Krückeberg and will forward the amount. The document is
issued from Bückeburg on 22 August 1849 and signed by Höner.

ad
N. Cam. 4731

Betr: Gesuch des Colons Krückeberg N. 10 in Berenbusch um Erlaubniß zum

Unter Semission des vom Colon Krückeberg N. 10 in Berenbusch
eingebrachten Gesuches, rubricirten Gegenstand betreffend, und unter
Bezugnahme auf das abschriftlich anliegende Protocoll vom gestrigen Tage
berichten wir, daß wir bei der Gewährung des Gesuches des p.p. Krückeberg
nichts zu erinnern befinden.

Wir bitten Fürstliche Rentkammer um baldige hohe Verfügung, da der Colon
Krückeberg gewillet ist, behufs seiner Übersiedelung nach Amerika schon
am 13. k. M. von hier abzureisen.

Zur Erledigung der Resolution vom 13. d. M. werden wir 12 mg
Stempelstrafe von ihm einziehen lassen und demnächst einzusenden
unverfehlen.

Bückeburg 22 August 1849

Höner

No. 4997

Presented at the Chancellery, August 23, 1849.
NC 7
Let consent be given to the sale of the holding.
Stamp duty recorded 23 August 1849.
Barkhausen

To the Princely Rent Chamber
from the Office of Bückeburg.

Protocol No. 1353.
to
Case No. 4731

Re: Petition of the Colon Krückeberg No. 10 in Berenbusch for permission
to sell his holding.

With the forwarding of the petition submitted by Colon Krückeberg No. 10
in Berenbusch, concerning the captioned matter, and with reference to the
protocol in copy attached from yesterday, we report that we have nothing
to object to the granting of said petition of Krückeberg.

We request that the Princely Rent Chamber issue its decision without
delay, as Colon Krückeberg is intending, for the purpose of his
relocation to America, to depart from here already on the 13th of the
coming month.

In execution of the resolution of the 13th of this month, we will collect
from him the 12 Mariengroschen stamp penalty and will submit it without
fail.

Bückeburg, 22 August 1849

Höner
