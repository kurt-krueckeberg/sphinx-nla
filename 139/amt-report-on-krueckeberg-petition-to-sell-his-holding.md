# Report of Amt Bückeburg on Petition of Colon Krückeberg No. 10 in Berenbusch for Permission to Sell his Farmstead (15 Sept. 1849)

## Narrative Summary

This document is a report from the Amt Bückeburg to the Princely Rentkammer
regarding the sale of Stätte (holding) No. 10 in Berenbusch. The Amt
states that, following receipt of the Kammer’s directive of August 23, the
purchase contract was formally drawn up and signed by both parties on
September 5. The Amt now forwards the completed contract to the Rentkammer
for official confirmation. The report also notes that the 12 Mariengroschen
stamp fee required in connection with the contract has been collected from
Colon Krückeberg and is being submitted along with the deed. The Amt
requests formal acknowledgment of the receipt of this payment. A marginal
note dated September 20 instructs that the purchase deed is to be confirmed
and returned once processed, and that proof of the dues and obligations
attached to the property is to be provided.

Report from the Office of Bückeburg

Subject: Petition of Colon Krückeberg No. 10 in Berenbusch for
permission to sell his holding.

After receipt of the Chamber official reply (rescript) of the 23rd of
last month, we proceeded to prepare the purchase contract and had it
executed by the contracting parties on the 5th of this month. We do not
fail to submit the contract herewith  for confirmation and at the same
time forward 12 Mariengroschen in stamp duty,† which we have collected
from Colon Krückeberg  and for the proper receipt of which  we ask to be
certified.

Bückeburg, 8 September 1849
Gröm

†are attached.

Lm
Arof.

[left margin:] To be reviewed (Probeten)

NC 7
 The deed of sale is to be confirmed and remitted without delay.
The encumbrances are to be shown. Issue receipt for 12 Mariengroschen,
together with collection authorization.
Bückeburg, 20 September 1849
Barkhausen.
