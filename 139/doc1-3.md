# Actenstück 1 (Administrative File Item 1): Draft of the Official Reply to the Widow Krückeberg (4 Aug. 1824)

## Summary

The is the **Draft (Concept) of the official reply (Rescript) to the widow
Krückeberg** It is the resolution to the petitioner: the prince has granted
her three years' service excemption from the **Burgrest labor days** and the
**forestry services**.

The text of the Office Reply appears on the top left (consisting of about
three lines of text) and on the top right of the image.

Translation

```{code-block} text

Concept

To the widow Krückeberg No. 10 in Bärenbusch.

By the highest authority, the widow Krückeberg, No. 10 in Bärenbusch,
has been graciously granted exemption from Burgrest and forestry service
for three years.

Bückeburg, 4 August 1824.
For the record.
A. Kaas
```
