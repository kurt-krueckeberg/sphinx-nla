# Glossary

```{list-table}
:header-rows: 1

* - Term (as in files)
  - Meaning (with notes & variants)
* - Auspfandung  (modern: Auspfändung)
  - Seizure/distress in the course of **enforcement** (levy on property). Often in formulas like **“die Auspfandung erkannt und vollzogen werden.”**
* - Bezugnahme
  - **Reference/cross-reference** to another order or letter, e.g., **unter Bezugnahme auf die Anweisung vom …** (“with reference to the instruction of …”).
* - Conc.
  - Abbrev. of **Concordat** (Latin **concordat** = “agrees/accords”). **Collation mark** indicating a copy agrees with the original; typically written next to or just above **initials of the verifying clerk**, often near the signature block.
* - Concept  (also: Konzept)
  - **Draft / office copy** kept on file (the fair copy would be issued out). Common as a heading: **Concept / Quittung.**
* - Courant  (abbr.: Cour.)
  - “Current” legal-tender **currency standard** (vs. **Banco**, **Species**). In sums like **“200 x[Rthlr.] Cour.”** → 200 Reichsthaler in current money.
* - Darlehen
  - **Loan** (sum advanced), frequently from the **Kammerkasse** (treasury).
* - Durchlaucht  (Sr. Durchlaucht)
  - Honorific **“Serene Highness”** (H.S.H.), used for a reigning prince.
* - Execution  (modern: Exekution)
  - **Enforcement of judgment** (compulsory execution). E.g., **“die Execution erkannt … den Amtsdiener … exequiren.”**
* - F.p.
  - This document has been officially recorded and put on file
* - Gesuch
  - **Petition / request** submitted to an office (often titled **Vorstellung** or **Gesuch**).
* - Groschen  (abbr.: gr / ggr)
  - Coin **subunit** used in sums (e.g., **“2 rt 12 gr”**). Exact value varied by state/period; treat as the small denomination under the **Reichsthaler**.
* - Grundstück
  - **Parcel/real property** (immovable). In these files often contrasted with movable goods in enforcement contexts.
* - Kammercasse  (modern: Kammerkasse)
  - The treasury **cash office** of the princely administration (part of the **Rentkammer**). Spelled with **Casse/Kasse** interchangeably in period docs.
* - Kaufleute
  - **Merchants / traders** (plural).
* - Länderei  (plural: Ländereien; variant: Ländereie)
  - **Landholding(s) / farmland.****Ländereie** is an archaic plural spelling you’ll see in older hands.
* - Nro.  (variants: Nr., No.)
  - Abbrev. of **Numero** (“number”). Used in headings/margins and by signatures to cite a **registry/protocol entry** or docket number (e.g., **Nro. 1274**). Often appears beside initials in the signature area.
* - Obligation
  - **Bond / promissory note** (sealed instrument acknowledging debt). Typically docketed by date and amount; on redemption it’s receipted and often **returned** to the debtor. Frequently synonymous with **Schuldverschreibung** in 19th-c. practice.
* - Quittung
  - **Receipt / acknowledgment of payment.** Appears as a heading or within text (**quittirt** = receipted).
* - Reichsthaler  (variants: Reichtsthaler; abbr.: Rthlr., Thlr., rt; sign: x)
  - Main **currency unit** in these files. Appears as **rt**, **Rthlr.**, **Thlr.**, or the taler sign **x** (e.g., **“200 x Cour.”**).
* - Rentcammer  (modern: Rentkammer)
  - The princely **finance/treasury department** (handles revenues, loans, obligations). Variant spellings with **c/k** are common.
* - Rentmeister  (Kammerrentmeister)
  - **Treasury officer** (cashier/controller) of the **Rentkammer**; e.g., **Kammerrentmeister Berger**.
* - Rescript  (German: Reskript; abbr.: Rescr., K. Res., Höchstes Rescr.)
  - Formal **written order/decision** issued by a higher authority (prince, council, or chamber). Often cited with date and addressee.
* - Schuldverschreibung
  - **Debt certificate/bond** (written promise to repay); often used interchangeably with **Obligation** in this period. May be secured by mortgage or pledges.
* - Verausgabung
  - **Disbursement/expenditure** from the treasury (cash paid out). **verausgabt** = disbursed/expended.
* - Verpflichtung
  - **Obligation/commitment** in the general legal sense (duty undertaken), not necessarily the sealed instrument (cf. **Obligation**, **Schuldverschreibung**).
* - Wiederankauf
  - **Repurchase/buy-back**, e.g., of land or rights; often appears with **Wiederankaufsrecht** (right of repurchase).
```
