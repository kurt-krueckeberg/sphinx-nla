# Docket Item 4: Draft of Official Order of the Chamber to Officer Creuzinger (25 Nov 1841)

## Overview

This page contains two administrative dispositions (Verfügungen) written
and signed by Reck—one expressing his opinion on the petition (a Votum),
and one ordering that a copy be sent to Officiant Creuzinger for further
report (an Abschriftsverfügung).

The first is Reck’s personal opinion (Votum). He records his view that:
although Krückeberg’s situation is unfortunate, granting him a loan is
impossible because the collateral is insufficient, and repurchasing or
selling the Söhren land is undesirable.

He concludes that the petition should simply remain rejected (“bei dem
abschlägigen Bescheid … bleiben”).

This was likely written in Reck’s capacity as a Kammer councillor during
the evaluation phase.

After recording his opinion, Reck directed that an official copy
(Abschriftlich) of the matter be sent to Officiant Creuzinger for a
“baldiger gutdienstlicher Bericht”, i.e., a prompt, service-related report
— specifically to obtain further information about:

- the quality and location of the land (Lage und Qualität des Landes), and
- the price it might fetch if sold.

This second note thus implements administrative follow-up. It doesn’t
contradict the first; it supplements it by initiating the next procedural
step.

  - So sad as Krückeberg’s situation may be, I still do not see how he
    can be helped. To approve a loan where the security is so
    insufficient is not possible. Just as unwelcome does it seem to me
    that the land in the Söhre which he purchased be bought back. If
    Krückeberg, on account of his debts, is at all able to dispose of
    it, let him seek to sell this land elsewhere. In my view, with the
    adverse decision it should simply remain as it is.
    Bückeburg, 25 Nov. 1841.
    
    Reck
    
    RC. Copy to Head Forester Kreutzinger for a prompt official report
    whether the supplicant’s land in the Söhre, considering its
    location and quality, is advisable to purchase and at what price
    it ought to be purchased.
    Bückeburg, 25 Nov. 1841.
    
    Reck
