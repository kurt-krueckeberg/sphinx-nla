```{code-block} text
Ans Amt Buckeburg Ausweisung

Das Amt wird beauftragt, den Krückeberg N. 10 zu Berenbusch zu wissen, daß
ihm die auf der Anlage erzeichnete Forstack, ist ohngefahr einen halben

Bbg. 13 Feb

[set of initials]

```

:::{admonition} Note on the Size of a Morgen

The Morgen was a traditional German unit of land measurement whose exact
size varied by region. In Schaumburg-Lippe in the early 19th century, one
Morgen generally corresponded to approximately 0.25 hectares (about 0.6
acres, or roughly 6,000 square meters). Accordingly, the half-Morgen
referenced in this document would have measured about 0.12–0.13 hectares
(approximately 0.3 acres, or about 3,000 square meters). Because the Morgen
was not a standardized unit, these figures represent approximate modern
equivalents.

:::

```{code-block} text
N.C. 62                                          3.)

C.

To the Office of Bückeburg
Land Assignment

The Office is instructed to inform Krückeberg No. 10 at Berenbusch that
the forest field marked on the attached plan, containing approximately
half a Morgen, is to be assigned to him upon payment of a reasonable price.

Before this, however, the Office shall report on any objections from the
holders of grazing rights (Hude rights), after hearing them.

Bückeburg, 13 Feb.

Dispathced by Schultz
```
