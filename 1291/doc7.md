```{code-block} text
:class: bordered

N.C. 16.
N.A. 749.
(Folio 7)

From the Amt of Bückeburg

Allotment.

On the basis of the report, the Amt is instructed to carry out the land
allotment mentioned therein for Einlieger (lodger/tenant) Vogt of Evesen
and thereafter to report back.

Bückeburg, 13 January 1818

Schnor
Spring

The allotment is to be carried out with the participation of the Chief
Forester and is to comprise an area of 1 to 1½ Morgen.

Expedited by: Alocis.
```
