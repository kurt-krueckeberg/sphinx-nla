## Transliteration and Translation

```{note}
The clerk incorrectly numbered this document as "20." in the upper
right corner instead of "21.";however, it is listed as a separate
document in the [Designatio Actorum](contents-list.md).
```

Receipt

```{code-block} text

Num: 458.                                20.)
Spring

Daß der Kolon Brüggemann N: 14 in Nordholz für
einen ausgewiesenen Platz 44 Rthrl. 15 gl. 4 d. in
Fürstl. Kammerkasse bezahlt hat quittirt.

Berger

Bückeburg
4 Nov. 1818

44 Rthrl. 15 gl. 4 d.
```

Translation of Receipt

```{code-block} text

No. 458.                                      20.)
Spring

That the Colon Brüggemann No. 14 in Nordholz has paid
for an assigned plot 44 Reichsthaler 15 Groschen 4 Pfennige
into the Princely Chamber Treasury is hereby receipted.

Berger

Bückeburg
4 November 1818

44 Reichsthaler 15 Groschen 4 Pfennige
```
