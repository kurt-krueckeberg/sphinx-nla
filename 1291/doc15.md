Translation

```{code-block} text

No. Cam. 386                                               15.)
Stamp: 3 Mariengroschen

To Neubauer Brüggemann No. 14 in Nordholz

[initials:]
v. Danckwarts
Winde
M. Kaas

Dispatched by: Knöttge

The request of Neubauer Brüggemann that the plot assigned to him for the
construction of a house be granted to him free of charge cannot be
approved; rather, payment for it must be made immediately.

Bückeburg, 7 October 1818
By Princely authority, etc.
[initials]

[uncertain]
```
