# Rentkammer Terminology

These terms appear in the case files’s Designatio Actorum (contents list) and throughout
the documents in the case file.

```{list-table} Terms Defined
:header-rows: 1

* - Term in case file
  - English explanation
* - Rentkammer
  - The territorial chamber responsible for fiscal administration, land tenure, state domains, forests, and payments in Schaumburg-Lippe; it functioned as both an administrative authority and a decision-making body for land allocations and fees.
* - Designatio Actorum
  - A formal register or contents list of documents in a case file, listing submissions, reports, resolutions, and receipts in chronological order.
* - Vorstellung
  - A formal petition or submission addressed to an authority, requesting a decision, approval, or action.
* - Rescript
  - A written decision or order issued by a superior authority in response to a petition or report.
* - Conc. K. Rescript
  - “Concluded Chancery Rescript”; a finalized written decision of the chancellery, formally issued and entered into the record.
* - Resolution
  - A formal ruling or determination made by the competent authority, often following review of petitions and reports.
* - Com. K. Resolution
  - “Communicated Chancery Resolution”; a resolution that has been officially transmitted to the concerned parties or local office.
* - Res: Cam:
  - Abbreviation for **Resolution der Cammer**; a decision issued by the Rentkammer.
* - Amt
  - Local administrative office responsible for implementing decisions, gathering reports, and supervising villages within its district.
* - hiesiges Amt
  - “The local office here”; refers to the Amt with jurisdiction over the matter at hand.
* - Forstdepartement
  - Administrative department responsible for state forests, forest land, and forestry matters.
* - Oberförster
  - Senior forester; a high-ranking forestry official responsible for oversight and reporting.
* - Zeichnung
  - A drawing or plan, usually depicting land, boundaries, or building sites.
* - Specification
  - A detailed written description or itemized list, often accompanying a drawing or report.
* - Kammercasse
  - The chamber treasury; the financial office responsible for receiving fees and issuing receipts.
* - Kammercassenquittung
  - A receipt issued by the chamber treasury acknowledging payment.
* - Ausweisung
  - The formal allocation or assignment of land, often state- or forest-owned, to private individuals.
* - Forstgrund
  - Forest land belonging to the state or territorial authority.
* - Neubauer
  - A newly established settler or smallholder granted a new holding or building site.
* - Einlieger
  - A lodger or dependent inhabitant without independent landholding, residing on another person’s property.
* - Colon
  - A hereditary or long-term leaseholder of a rural holding under territorial law.
* - No. (Hausnummer)
  - The official house or holding number within a village.
```
